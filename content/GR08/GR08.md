## Grade 8 Mathematics curriculum outline

### Term 1

#### Topic 1: Numbers and Operations

* Integers and decimal numbers
* Order of operations
* Properties of operations
* Simplifying expressions
* Evaluating expressions

#### Topic 2: Algebra

* Introduction to algebra
* Simplifying algebraic expressions
* Solving linear equations
* Graphing linear equations

### Term 2

#### Topic 3: Geometry and Measurement

* Points, lines, and planes
* Angles and properties of angles
* Congruent and similar triangles
* Perimeter and area of triangles
* Surface area and volume of 3D shapes

#### Topic 4: Data Analysis and Graphing

* Introduction to data analysis
* Types of data
* Graphing data
* Interpreting data from graphs

### Term 3

#### Topic 5: Probability

* Introduction to probability
* Experimental probability
* Theoretical probability
* Probability of compound events

#### Topic 6: Review and Assessment

* Review of all topics
* Assessment of student understanding
